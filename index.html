How to Use

  • Title Tag: Enter your page title. Google typically cuts off titles around 600px. Ideally, keep it under 60 characters.
  • Meta Description: Write a compelling summary. Desktop limit is ~960px (~155 chars), mobile is shorter.
  • Pixels vs Characters: Google truncates based on pixel width, not character count. "W" is wider than "i". This tool calculates exact pixels.
  • View Modes: Toggle between Desktop and Mobile to see how your snippet appears on different devices.
